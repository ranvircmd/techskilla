Who We Are

Sanah Internatinal Private Limited of Opportunities

Sanah Internatinal is a Private Limited Compnay registered in India, dedicated to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

We are a professional academic facilitation organization that connects students, colleges, industries, and NGOs through structured internships, skill training, research exposure, and career-oriented certification programs aligned with NEP 2020 (National Education Policy).

Our name "Sanah Internatinal Private Limited" signifies our commitment to creating a vast ocean of creative opportunities where students can explore, learn, and grow. We believe every student deserves access to quality experiential learning that prepares them for real-world challenges.
