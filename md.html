About Ms. Damini Kumari

Ms. Damini Kumari is the visionary Managing Director of RIGHTNEW LLP, bringing exceptional expertise in rural development, governance, and educational leadership. With a strong academic foundation and a passion for empowering students, she leads the organization's strategic initiatives to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

Damini has a good mix of creativity and hard work. Her areas of interest include empowerment of women and livelihood where she has also done considerable amount of work over the tenure of this course. She is an enthusiast in fields of dancing and painting. Research Project: "Impact of Livelihood Intervention on Rural Dalit Women: A Case Study of Socio-Political and Economical Spheres in Muzaffarpur Disrict, Bihar" Experiential Learning 1: Understanding the Political, Economic and Social Structures in Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh. Experiential Learning 2: Interned with APMAS, Bihar and did a project on functioning and structure of SHGs in rural areas, with special focus on Community Participation, Capacity Building and Women Empowerment. Languages Known: English, Hindi, Magahi, Angika and Bhojpuri.
