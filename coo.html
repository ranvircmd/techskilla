About Mr. Chandan Khargaha

Chandan Khargaha is a dynamic business leader, entrepreneur, and strategic operations expert with a strong academic foundation in B.Tech and MBA. With a diverse career spanning corporate leadership, academia, and entrepreneurship, he brings a unique blend of industry insight and institutional experience.

He has worked with leading organizations such as Airtel, Snapdeal, and Zomato, where he gained extensive exposure to operations management, business development, marketing strategy, and large-scale execution. His corporate journey has equipped him with a deep understanding of market dynamics, customer behavior, and scalable business models.

Transitioning into leadership roles, Chandan served as a Director in an engineering college for over 5 years, where he played a key role in academic administration, industry-academia collaboration, skill development initiatives, and institutional growth. His contribution significantly enhanced student engagement, employability programs, and organizational efficiency.

As a passionate entrepreneur and startup mentor, he has guided emerging businesses and young professionals in areas such as business strategy, operations setup, digital growth, and career development. His vision is centered on building sustainable, impact-driven ventures.

Currently serving as the Managing Director at Sanah International Private Limited , Chandan leads the company’s operations across multiple domains including manpower solutions, marketing research, skill development, internships, and R&D initiatives. He is responsible for strategic planning, process optimization, partnerships, and scaling business operations.

Known for his leadership, execution excellence, and innovative thinking, Chandan Khargaha is committed to driving organizational success while creating meaningful opportunities for students, professionals, and institutions.
