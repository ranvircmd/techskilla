Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Introduction

Sanah International Private Limited is a professional academic facilitation organization providing internships, skill development, and training programs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • City / Location
  • College / Educational Details
  • Course and Specialization
  • Skills and Preferences
  • Any information submitted through forms

We may also automatically collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage behavior.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process internship applications
  • To communicate via email, phone, or WhatsApp
  • To provide training and services
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To send updates, offers, or notifications (if consent is given)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, your information may be shared with:

  • Partner organizations for internship opportunities
  • Service providers for operational purposes
  • Authorities if required by law

5. WhatsApp & Communication

When you Privacy-Policy or submit forms, your data may be used to communicate via WhatsApp or phone for faster support and updates.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for providing services, internship processing, and legal compliance.

9. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of data
  • Request deletion of your data

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms such as WhatsApp and social media. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age.

12. Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
